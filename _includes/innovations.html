A beautifully designed framework that makes it easy to connect analytics and actions to video/image feeds

Industry Verticals:

  • Retail inventory analysis
  • Smart traffic cameras
  • Enteprise IoT device monitoring
  • Corporate and home security & surveillance

Key Aspects:

  • Simple. Build in days or weeks. Not months or years
  • Three layers of abstraction:
    • Core C++ layer for embedded systems
    • Javascript adaptation for NodeJS and browsers
    • Full featured React based UI for NVR/CCTV/surveillance
  • Multiple built-in people/feature/shape detection mechanisms
  • Plug-in your favorite 3rd party libraries easily (license plate analysis, drone detection)
  • Built in notification for alarms/events, video recording and storage
  • Our secret sauce is our architecture. Have a cool idea? Write a component, plug it into our system
